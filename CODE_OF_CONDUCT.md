# Quantera Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Quantera is committed to building institutional-grade financial infrastructure. We believe that a respectful, inclusive community is essential to achieving this mission. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all participants in our community, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Professionalism**: Communicate with the same level of professionalism expected in institutional finance
- **Respect**: Treat all community members with respect, regardless of background or experience level
- **Constructive Feedback**: Provide thoughtful, actionable feedback focused on improving the project
- **Collaboration**: Work together openly, sharing knowledge and supporting fellow contributors
- **Accountability**: Take responsibility for your contributions and their impact on the project
- **Security Mindset**: Report security vulnerabilities responsibly through proper channels

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior of any kind
- Personal attacks, trolling, or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam, excessive self-promotion, or off-topic content
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Attempting to exploit or manipulate the platform or its users

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Quantera community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Official communication channels (Discord, forums, etc.)
- Community events and meetups
- Direct communications related to project work

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Email**: conduct@quantera.finance
2. **GitHub**: Open a confidential issue with repository maintainers

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

### Consequences

Community leaders will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:

1. **Correction**: Private written warning with clarity around the violation
2. **Warning**: Public warning with consequences for continued behavior
3. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary suspension from community interaction
4. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from all community spaces

### Appeals

Participants may appeal enforcement decisions by contacting the project maintainers at appeals@quantera.finance within 14 days of the decision.
